How can we help?

Common questions about using DevOpsHub AI.

Is DevOpsHub AI really free?▼

Yes — every tool, generator, and the AI assistant are completely free with no sign-up required. Everything runs directly in your browser.

Do I need to create an account?▼

No. There are no accounts, logins, or saved data on our servers. Each tool works statelessly — generate what you need and copy or download it.

Is my data sent anywhere?▼

Generators (Docker, K8s, Terraform, etc.) run 100% locally in your browser — nothing is sent anywhere. The AI Assistant sends your message to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a response, the same as any AI chat tool.

Can I use the generated YAML/Dockerfiles in production?▼

The generators produce solid starting templates following common best practices (multi-stage builds, resource limits, health probes). Always review and adjust for your specific security and scaling requirements before production use.

The dashboard numbers look fake — are they real metrics?▼

Correct — the Monitoring Dashboard is a simulation for demonstration and learning purposes. It is not connected to any real infrastructure.

I found a bug or want to suggest a tool. Where do I report it?▼

Open an issue on our GitHub repository, or reach out via the contact links in the footer.
